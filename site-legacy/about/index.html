About the Learning Engine

The Cosmogenesis Learning Engine is a world-building generator for Codex 144:99. It assembles layered geometry, research registries, and interactive stories while preserving ND-safe pacing and provenance.

What it hopes to achieve

Give artists, educators, and ritual designers a calm toolkit for exploring sacred correspondences. Every mode — Art, Learn, Play, and Study — derives from the same registry data so changes remain consistent across the ecosystem.

ND-safe pledge

No autoplay, no strobe, no forced motion. Controls stay in the visitor's hands, color palettes favor gentle contrast, and every scene includes opt-out paths.

Read the covenant in Markdown form at /docs/covenant/covenant.md.

How bridges connect

  • tesseract-bridge — heavy geometry baking and ECS meshes.
  • liber-arcanae — tarot overlays and advanced choose-your-own-adventure logic.
  • data-ingest-pipeline — OCR and PDF-to-JSON tooling for new research scrolls.

These bridges stay optional. The core repo runs offline without them.
